Privacy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

What this site collects

The Nodex landing site is a static site. It does not use first-party analytics, advertising trackers, or account creation.

Download and outbound links

When you click download or repository links, you leave this site and go to third-party services such as GitHub and Homebrew. Those services may collect network logs, browser metadata, and any information you choose to provide there.

Cookies

This site does not set first-party cookies. GitHub Pages and linked destinations may apply their own infrastructure or security controls.

Contact and updates

If the privacy posture of the site changes, this page will be updated alongside the source in the public repository.
